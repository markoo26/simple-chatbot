# Booking Process - KRK Airport Parking

## How to Book Your Parking Space

We offer multiple convenient ways to secure your parking space at KRK Airport Parking. Follow the steps below for a smooth booking experience.

---

## Online Booking (Recommended)

### Step 1: Visit Our Website
Go to **www.krkairportparking.pl** and click the "Book Now" button on the homepage.

### Step 2: Enter Your Details
Fill in the booking form with the following information:

- **Arrival Date & Time:** When you plan to drop off your vehicle
- **Departure Date & Time:** When you plan to pick up your vehicle
- **Vehicle Information:**
  - License plate number
  - Make and model (e.g., Toyota Corolla, VW Golf)
  - Vehicle dimensions (to confirm it meets our size requirements)
  - Color
- **Parking Preference:**
  - Standard Uncovered (€5-15/day)
  - Premium Uncovered (€15-30/day)
  - Covered (€30-50/day)
- **Additional Services (Optional):**
  - Car wash (€15 exterior, €30 full detail)
  - Electric vehicle charging (€5/day)

### Step 3: Review Pricing
The system will automatically calculate your total price based on:
- Number of days (partial days count as full days)
- Parking type selected
- Any additional services

**Pricing Example:**
- 5 days + 3 hours = 6 full days charged
- Standard uncovered at €10/day = €60 total

### Step 4: Create an Account or Continue as Guest
- **Create Account:** Save your details for faster future bookings, view booking history
- **Guest Checkout:** Quick one-time booking without registration

### Step 5: Add Contact Information
- Full name
- Email address (booking confirmation will be sent here)
- Phone number (for SMS updates and emergency contact)
- Optional: Flight number (we can track delays and adjust shuttle timing)

### Step 6: Payment
Choose your preferred payment method:

- **Credit/Debit Card:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **Online Transfer:** Przelewy24, PayU
- **BLIK:** Polish mobile payment system
- **PayPal:** International customers welcome

**Payment Security:** All transactions are encrypted with SSL/TLS. We never store your full card details.

### Step 7: Confirmation
- Instant email confirmation with booking reference number
- SMS confirmation with directions and entry code
- Add to calendar option (Google Calendar, iCal)
- Download PDF booking voucher

**Your booking confirmation includes:**
- Unique booking ID (e.g., KRK-2024-00123)
- Entry QR code for automatic barrier access
- Parking space assignment (assigned 24 hours before arrival)
- Shuttle schedule information
- Contact details for support

## Cancellation Policy

We understand plans change. Here's our fair cancellation policy:

### Full Refund
- Cancel **more than 48 hours** before scheduled arrival
- 100% refund processed within 5-7 business days
- No questions asked

### Partial Refund
- Cancel **24-48 hours** before arrival: 50% refund
- Cancellation fee: €10 administrative charge

### No Refund
- Cancel **less than 24 hours** before arrival: No refund
- No-show without cancellation: No refund

**How to Cancel:**
- Online: Through your account dashboard
- Phone: +48 12 345 6789
- Email: cancellations@krkairportparking.pl (include booking reference)

**Refund Processing:**
- Refunds are returned to original payment method
- Processing time: 5-7 business days (may take up to 14 days depending on your bank)

## Check-in Process

### Arrival at Our Facility

**Step 1: Approach the Entrance**
- Drive up to the yellow automatic barrier
- Have your QR code ready (from confirmation email/SMS)

**Step 2: Barrier Access**
- **Option A:** Scan QR code at the reader (barrier opens automatically)
- **Option B:** Press the call button - our staff will verify your booking and open the barrier
- **Option C:** Enter your booking reference number on the keypad

**Step 3: Proceed to Reception**
- Park temporarily in the designated "Check-in Zone" (spaces #1-3)
- Enter the reception building

**Step 4: Complete Check-in**
Our staff will:
- Verify your booking and ID
- Conduct a quick exterior vehicle inspection (photos for your protection)
- Assign your parking space number
- Provide shuttle schedule information
- Answer any questions

**Time required:** 5-10 minutes

**Step 5: Park Your Vehicle**
- Drive to your assigned parking space
- Lock your vehicle
- Keep one key with you, leave spare key at reception (optional but recommended in case of emergency)

**Step 6: Shuttle to Airport**
- Return to the shuttle pickup area
- Board the next available shuttle (see schedule in Location section)
- 3-5 minute ride to terminal

---

## Check-out Process

### Returning from Your Trip

**Step 1: Call for Shuttle Pickup**
Once you land and collect your luggage:
- Call +48 12 345 6789 or WhatsApp +48 600 123 456
- Or simply go to Shuttle Stop P5 at the airport (see Location section for details)

**Step 2: Shuttle Ride**
- Our shuttle will pick you up within 10-15 minutes
- 3-5 minute ride back to our facility

**Step 3: Reception Check-out**
- Visit reception desk
- Present your booking confirmation or ID
- Staff will retrieve your keys (if you left a spare)
- Review your final bill (any additional charges for extended stay, services, etc.)

**Step 4: Pay Any Additional Fees**
- If you stayed longer than booked, pay the difference
- Payment for any extra services (car wash, charging)
- Accept cash, card, BLIK

**Step 5: Vehicle Inspection**
- Collect your vehicle from your parking space
- Quick inspection to ensure everything is in order
- Report any issues immediately (covered by our facility insurance)

**Step 6: Exit**
- Drive to exit barrier
- Barrier opens automatically after checkout is complete
- Safe travels!

**Total checkout time:** 10-15 minutes

---

## Early Arrival or Late Departure

### Returning Earlier Than Planned
- No problem! No penalty for early pickup
- You will still be charged for the full booked period (no partial refunds for unused days)

### Staying Longer Than Booked

**Option 1: Modify Booking in Advance**
- Extend your booking online or by phone before your original end date
- Pay the difference at current rates

**Option 2: Pay Upon Return**
- Extra days will be charged at the standard daily rate for your parking type
- Payment due at checkout
- Subject to space availability (during peak season, we may need to relocate your vehicle)

**Overstay Fees:**
- First 6 hours over: Free grace period
- After 6 hours: Charged as full additional day
- We'll send SMS/email reminder on your scheduled checkout day

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I book same-day parking?**  
A: Yes, subject to availability. We recommend calling us first at +48 12 345 6789 to confirm space availability.

**Q: Do I need to print my confirmation?**  
A: No, a digital copy (email or SMS) is sufficient. We can look up your booking with just your name and phone number.

**Q: Can someone else drop off or pick up my car?**  
A: Yes, just note this in your booking and provide their name and phone number. They'll need to show ID.

**Q: What if my flight is delayed?**  
A: No problem! We operate 24/7. Just let us know if your pickup time changes significantly. The 6-hour grace period covers most delays.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**  
A: Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and comply with PCI DSS standards. We never store full card details.
