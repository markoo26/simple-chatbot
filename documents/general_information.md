# General Information - KRK Airport Parking

## About Our Facility

Welcome to KRK Airport Parking, your trusted parking solution near Kraków John Paul II International Airport. We are a privately-owned parking facility dedicated to providing secure, convenient, and affordable parking for travelers flying out of Kraków.

## Capacity and Vehicle Requirements

Our facility offers **100 secure parking spaces** designed exclusively for regular-sized passenger vehicles. We can accommodate cars up to the following dimensions:

- **Maximum Length:** 5.0 meters
- **Maximum Width:** 2.0 meters
- **Maximum Height:** 2.1 meters

**Please Note:** We cannot accommodate vans, SUVs larger than the specified dimensions, minibuses, trucks, camper vans, or commercial vehicles. If you're unsure whether your vehicle fits our requirements, please contact us before booking.

## Proximity to Airport

Located just **1 kilometer walking distance** from the KRK Airport terminal, our parking facility is one of the closest off-site options available. For those who prefer not to walk or are traveling with heavy luggage, we offer a complimentary shuttle service (see below).

## Free Shuttle Service

We provide a **free shuttle bus service** running between our parking facility and the airport terminal:

- Available 24/7 for all customers
- Runs every 15-20 minutes during peak hours (4:00 AM - 10:00 PM)
- Runs every 30 minutes during night hours (10:00 PM - 4:00 AM)
- Journey time: approximately 3-5 minutes
- Perfect for travelers with heavy luggage, families with children, or anyone who prefers not to walk
- No advance booking required - simply inform our staff upon arrival
