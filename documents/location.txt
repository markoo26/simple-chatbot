## How to Find Us

### From Kraków City Center (15 km)

**By Car (approximately 20-25 minutes):**

1. Head west on al. Mickiewicza toward Rondo Mogilskie
2. Continue onto al. 29 Listopada
3. Merge onto A4 motorway toward Katowice/Balice
4. Take exit 145 toward Balice/Airport (KRK)
5. Turn right onto ul. Kpt. M. Medweckiego
6. Turn left onto ul. Balicka
7. KRK Airport Parking will be on your right after 300 meters
8. Look for our large blue and white sign: "KRK AIRPORT PARKING"

**Public Transportation:**
- Train: Take the train from Kraków Główny to "Kraków Airport" station (18 minutes), then 8-minute walk or contact us for shuttle pickup
- Bus: Line 208 or 209 from city center to "Balice Lotnisko" stop, then 5-minute walk

### From A4 Motorway (East - Warsaw Direction)

1. Take exit 145 toward Balice/Lotnisko
2. At the roundabout, take the 2nd exit onto ul. Kpt. M. Medweckiego
3. Turn left onto ul. Balicka
4. Parking facility is 300m on your right

### From A4 Motorway (West - Katowice Direction)

1. Take exit 145 toward Balice/Airport
2. Turn right onto ul. Kpt. M. Medweckiego
3. Turn left onto ul. Balicka
4. Parking facility is 300m on your right

## Walking Route to Airport

For those who prefer to walk to the terminal:

**Route Description:**
1. Exit our parking facility and turn right onto ul. Balicka
2. Walk 300m and turn left onto ul. Kpt. M. Medweckiego
3. Continue straight for 700m
4. Airport terminal will be directly ahead
5. **Total time:** 12-15 minutes at a normal walking pace

**Path Features:**
- Well-lit pedestrian sidewalk entire route
- Covered walkway for final 200m approach to terminal
- Safe pedestrian crossings at all intersections
- Wheelchair accessible route

**Recommended For:**
- Travelers with light luggage (backpack or small carry-on)
- Those wanting a bit of exercise before a long flight
- Good weather conditions
- Daytime travel
