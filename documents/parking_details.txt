# Parking Details - KRK Airport Parking

## Parking Spaces and Layout

Our facility features **100 numbered parking spaces** arranged in a well-organized layout for easy navigation:

- **Covered Parking:** 40 spaces (Spaces #81-100 are covered, plus 20 premium covered spots)
- **Uncovered Parking:** 60 spaces (Spaces #1-80, excluding covered premium)
- **Space Dimensions:** Each space is 2.5m wide × 5.5m long
- **Surface Type:** Paved asphalt surface, well-maintained and regularly resurfaced
- **Accessibility:** Wide driveways (6 meters) for easy maneuvering

## Parking Categories

### Standard Uncovered Parking
- Spaces #1-60
- Asphalt surface
- Full CCTV coverage
- Budget-friendly option
- Starting from €5/day

### Premium Uncovered Parking
- Spaces #61-80
- Located closest to the shuttle pickup point
- Priority shuttle boarding during peak times

### Covered Parking
- Spaces #81-100
- Protected from weather (rain, snow, sun)
- Additional security with restricted access
- Premium service with priority shuttle
- Starting from €30/day

## Security Features

Your vehicle's safety is our top priority:

- **24/7 CCTV Surveillance:** Over 30 high-definition cameras covering every parking space and entrance/exit point
- **Perimeter Fencing:** Entire facility is secured with 2.5-meter fencing
- **Access Control:** Electronic barrier system with ticket/booking validation
- **Security Personnel:** Trained security staff on-site around the clock
- **Motion Sensors:** Additional sensors in covered parking areas
- **Regular Patrols:** Security walks every 2 hours throughout the facility
- **Fire Safety:** Fire extinguishers and smoke detectors in covered areas

## Vehicle Size Restrictions (Important!)

For safety and efficient use of space, we enforce strict vehicle size limits:

**Maximum Dimensions:**
- Length: 5.0 meters (16.4 feet)
- Width: 2.0 meters (6.6 feet)
- Height: 2.1 meters (6.9 feet)

**Examples of Acceptable Vehicles:**
- Sedans (Toyota Corolla, VW Passat, BMW 3 Series)
- Small SUVs (Toyota RAV4, Honda CR-V, Mazda CX-5)
- Hatchbacks (VW Golf, Ford Focus, Renault Clio)
- Compact cars (Fiat 500, Mini Cooper, Smart ForTwo)

**Vehicles NOT Accepted:**
- Large SUVs (Cadillac Escalade, Range Rover Sport)
- Vans and minivans (VW Transporter, Mercedes Sprinter)
- Pickup trucks (Ford F-150, Dodge RAM)
- Camper vans or RVs
- Vehicles with roof boxes exceeding height limit
- Commercial vehicles or trucks

## Parking Terms and Conditions

- Minimum parking period: 1 day (partial days charged as full days)
- Maximum vehicle value coverage: €50,000 (additional insurance available)
- Prohibited items: Flammable materials, weapons, illegal substances
- Smoking is strictly prohibited throughout the facility
- Pets must remain in vehicles (except service animals)
- Management reserves the right to refuse vehicles that don't meet size requirements

## Maintenance Schedule

For your awareness, our facility undergoes regular maintenance:
- **Daily:** Cleaning, trash removal, lighting checks
- **Weekly:** Pressure washing of driveways, landscaping
- **Monthly:** CCTV system inspection, barrier system maintenance
- **Quarterly:** Asphalt inspection and repair, security audit

**Note:** Maintenance activities do not affect parking availability or access to your vehicle.
